About Mitticraft India

Preserving tradition. Empowering artisans. Celebrating handmade heritage.

Our Story

Mitticraft India was founded with a vision to revive traditional mud art and bring handcrafted beauty into modern homes. We blend heritage, creativity, and passion to preserve India’s rich artistic culture.

The Craft

Our art is inspired by traditional Lippan Mud Work and mirror detailing. Each piece is carefully shaped, designed, and finished by hand, making every creation unique and soulful.

Our Artisans

We collaborate with skilled rural artisans who have inherited this craft across generations. By supporting Mitticraft India, you directly support their livelihood and cultural preservation.
