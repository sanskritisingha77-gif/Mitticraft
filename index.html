About Our Craft

Elevate your space with the shimmering geometry of Lippan mud art. This ancestral craft uses natural clay to create intricate, three-dimensional textures that play with shadow and light. Hand-embedded mirrors cast a warm, dancing glow across the room.

Mitticraft India celebrates sustainability, tradition, and artisan empowerment across India.
